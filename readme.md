# 🏏 IPL Match Performance Analysis (2008–2024)

> End-to-end Data Analytics project uncovering performance trends,
> strategic insights, and match predictions from 16 seasons of IPL cricket.

---

## Problem Statement
What factors most influence match outcomes in the IPL,
and which teams, venues, and players consistently outperform?

---

## Key Findings

| Insight | Finding |
|---------|---------|
| Most dominant team | Mumbai Indians |
| Optimal toss call | Field first — wins 53%+ of matches |
| Best chasing team | Mumbai Indians |
| Most used venue | Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai |
| Highest impact player | AB de Villiers / Chris Gayle |
| Best under pressure | Mumbai Indians (most close match wins) |

---

## Analysis Covered

- ✅ Team win rates and win percentages
- ✅ Batting first vs chasing performance
- ✅ Home vs away advantage
- ✅ Toss impact by season and venue
- ✅ Season-by-season performance trends
- ✅ Venue intelligence (scoring, chase win %, best teams)
- ✅ Player of the Match analysis and consistency
- ✅ Full head-to-head matrix for top 8 teams
- ✅ Close match and win margin analysis
- ✅ Logic-based match outcome predictor
